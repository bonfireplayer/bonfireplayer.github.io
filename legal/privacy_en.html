Data Processing for Third-Party Advertising Services 4.1 Advertising SDK Integration Status: This software has officially integrated the third-party advertising service provider: Google AdMob SDK. When you use specific features within this software (such as creation synthesis and dubbing lock removal), the SDK will operate and process relevant data. 4.2 Data Scope Obtained by the Advertising SDK:To count ad impressions, prevent malicious click fraud, and optimize ad loading efficiency, the Google AdMob SDK may automatically obtain or process the following data: Device Anonymous Advertising Identifiers: Including the Google Advertising ID (GAID) and OAID on Android, and the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS. These identifiers are used to distinguish independent devices for ad serving (the acquisition of certain identifiers depends on your system-level authorization); Network and Location Information: Including your IP address. This information is used to estimate your approximate geographical location (city level) to deliver advertisements that comply with local regulations and language environments; Device and Application Technical Specifications: Including device model, operating system version, application version number, and screen resolution, used to ensure perfect ad compatibility and rendering across different devices; Ad Interaction Records: Including ad impressions, clicks, and the completion status of rewarded video ads (used to issue rewards such as dubbing quotas). 4.3 Data Processing and Privacy Notes: Direct Transmission: The aforementioned data is directly encrypted and transmitted by the Google AdMob SDK to Google's advertising servers via device system APIs. It does not pass through, nor is it stored on, our application servers; Anonymization: The data processed consists entirely of device-level anonymous technical data, which does not contain and cannot be linked to your real name, phone number, account, or any other sensitive privacy information that could identify your real-world identity; User Control: You can choose to "Limit Ad Tracking," "Reset Advertising Identifier," or deny IDFA/GAID tracking permissions through your mobile device's system settings at any time.
